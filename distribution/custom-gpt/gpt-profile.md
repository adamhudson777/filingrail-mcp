# SEC Filing Analyst — GPT profile fields

## Name
SEC Filing Analyst

## Description (for the GPT card, <300 chars)
Ask about any U.S. public company's SEC filings and get real numbers, not guesses. Financials, insider trades, 8-K events, and 13F holdings — every figure linked back to its exact sec.gov filing. Powered by the Filingrail SEC EDGAR API.

## Category (GPT Store)

## Conversation starters
- What was Apple's revenue and operating income last quarter?
- Show me insider selling at NVIDIA in the last 30 days.
- What 8-K events did Tesla file this month, and what were they about?
- List Berkshire Hathaway's 10 largest 13F holdings.
